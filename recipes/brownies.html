Brownies Recipe: A Sweet Treat for Everyone!

Brownies, the quintessential treat for any occasion, beckon with their irresistible allure of decadence and indulgence. Homemade brownies, baked with precision and passion, boast a fudgy interior that melts in the mouth, complemented by a crackly crust that adds a satisfying crunch. Rich cocoa flavors mingle with notes of butter and sugar, creating a symphony of sweetness that delights the senses. Whether studded with chunks of chocolate, nuts, or swirls of caramel, each brownie is a bite-sized delight that promises pure bliss with every nibble. Fresh from the oven, the aroma of warm chocolate fills the air, evoking memories of childhood and simple pleasures. With their irresistible charm and timeless appeal, homemade brownies are a culinary treasure that brings joy to any gathering, leaving a lasting impression on all who partake.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 4 eggs
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon baking powderr
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup walnut halves

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch pan.
  2. Mix sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl until combined. Spread the batter into the prepared pan. Decorate with walnut halves.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until top is dry and edges have started to pull away from the sides of the pan, about 20 to 30 minutes; cool before slicing into squares.
